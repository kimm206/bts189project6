Learn about the Korean American Coalition (KAC) and our newest initiatives

Our yearly convention is one of KAC National's signature nationals events to promote our service work to the communities we serve.
All are welcome: new members, current members, board members, volunteers, or any professional who is interested in our organization.

We are a community made of 1st - 2nd - and beyond generation immigrants, adoptees, refugees, and supporters of Korean-Americans.

The Korean American Coalition (KAC) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization established in 1983 to promote the civic and civil rights interests of the Korean American community.
We have 8 current chapters nationwide and are looking to expand our presence.
